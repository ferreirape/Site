Elenda, the Dust Rose

Legendary Creature — Vampire Knight

Lifelink Whenever another creature dies, put a +1/+1 counter on Elenda, the Dusk Rose. When Elenda dies, create X 1/1 white Vampire creature tokens with lifelink, where X is Elenda’s power. Her centuries-long search is ended. 1/1

Black / White


With the power of white mana, the deck has the synergy of strengthening creatures and gaining life.
With the power of black mana, the synergy is to take advantage of the death of creatures.

Deck List

White and black deck, in the style of aristocrat, with the mechanic of generating counters in vampire-type creatures, in this way making plays to gain life points and the opponent loses them.
